Kanban

Visual project management built around the way work actually flows. Drag cards between lists, assign members, set due dates, and attach notes directly to cards. Boards can be shared with fine-grained read/write permissions — send invite links directly to email so collaborators can join without an account. Whether you're running a solo project or coordinating a team, Kanban gives you a clear, living picture of everything in flight.
